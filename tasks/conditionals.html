Conditionals - Overview

Conditionals like - if - if else - switches

  • Different types of '=' with meanings/descriptions
  • 1 equal sign that's usually an assignment
  • 2 equal sign is comparing
  • 3 euqal sign is comapring too, except that the two data types have to match
